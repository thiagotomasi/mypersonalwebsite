Know more about me

I'm a product focused designer based in Helsinki, Finland. Currently working at ABB as Lead Product Designer.

In my 10+ years I worked at companies developing and designing services looking at the whole picture, from the conception to validation, from business strategy to product emotions.

I had the privilege to work with brands like Coca Cola, John Deere, Ghost.org, Stihl, Interaction Design Foundation, Bienal and RBS Group. For clients in Brazil, Ireland, Denmark, USA, Belgium and Finland.
